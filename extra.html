About Me

Greek Heritage

Greek is my first language and shapes how I think about hospitality, community, and care. Growing up in a Greek household in New York means you learn early that how you treat people — at a table, in a meeting, in a product — says everything. That sensibility runs through everything I do.

Fluent in Greek (native proficiency).

Building Things

Outside of school and work I spend a lot of time building software products independently. Not for a grade or a title — just because the problem-solving is genuinely addictive. There’s a specific satisfaction in watching something you designed alone actually work end-to-end. I want to keep chasing that at scale.

Eagle Scout

Earned the rank of Eagle Scout in June 2021 — the highest rank in Scouts BSA, achieved by fewer than 4% of participants. The capstone project requirement (plan, fund, and lead a community service project from scratch) was my first real taste of what it means to own an outcome completely.

New York City

Lifelong New Yorker. I’ve worked across the city — Midtown agencies, Tribeca event venues, Queens construction offices. The city calibrates your standards in ways that are hard to replicate anywhere else.
